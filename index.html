ॐ श्री कृष्णाय नमः

"जय श्री कृष्ण" - Eternal Divine Beauty

Bhagwat Geeta Shlokas

कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥

Translation: You have a right to perform your prescribed duty, but you are not entitled to the fruits of action.

योगस्थः कुरु कर्माणि सङ्गं त्यक्त्वा धनञ्जय।
सिद्ध्यसिद्ध्योः समो भूत्वा समत्वं योग उच्यते॥

Translation: Perform your duty equipoised, O Arjuna, abandoning all attachment to success or failure.

ॐ कृष्णाय वासुदेवाय हरि: परमात्मने प्रणत: ।
कलेश नाशाय गोविन्दाय नमो नम: ॥

Removes all sorrows and grants divine peace

Divine Wisdom of Shree Krishna

"The soul is never born nor dies at any time. It has never been created, nor will it ever be destroyed."
- Bhagwat Geeta 2.20
"Whatever happened, happened for good. Whatever is happening, is happening for good."
- Krishna's Teaching
"Set thy heart upon thy work, but never on its reward."
- Bhagwat Geeta 2.47
"Fearlessness, purity of heart, steadfastness in knowledge and yoga."
- Qualities of Devotee
